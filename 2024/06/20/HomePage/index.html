About Me

My name is Ruan Kunming, and I am currently a data engineer, primarily working on data warehousing. Outside of work, I enjoy fishing, and so far, the largest fish I’ve caught weighed about 2kg. In 2013, I obtained a bachelor’s degree in Physics from Wuhan University, after which I studied at the Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I earned a graduate degree in 2016. By chance, I shifted from my original plan of pursuing a career in quantitative finance to the internet industry, where I have been working ever since.
